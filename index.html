Exploring Vietnam

A visual journal of moments captured in Vietnam

My name is Haifa, and I’m a designer with a deep interest in visual storytelling. During my travels across Vietnam, I’ve been documenting the country’s diverse landscapes, architecture, and cultural moments. This project is a visual journal — an attempt to capture the beauty I’ve encountered through design and thoughtful observation.

Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ha Long Bay is renowned for its limestone karsts and tranquil emerald waters, offering a remarkable coastal experience.
